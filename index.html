Kakashi Hatake's Profile

The Sixth Hokage

Kakashi Hatake is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Hatake clan. Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan, he is one of Konoha's most talented ninja, regularly looked to for advice and leadership despite his personal dislike of responsibility.

Records

Sensei

Team 7

To his students on Team 7, Kakashi teaches the importance of teamwork, a lesson he received, along with the Sharingan, from his childhood friend, Obito Uchiha. Team 7 consists of Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno.
